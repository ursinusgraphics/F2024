Step 2: fillCircle

Fill in the method fillCircle to fill in a solid circle, using only the drawPixel method. Note that that a point (x, y) is inside of a circle of radius r at a center of (cx, cy) if

\[ (x-cx)^2 + (y-cy)^2 < r^2 \]
